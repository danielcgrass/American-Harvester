1636

In 1636, America's first farm was born. The farm itself is still in operation today and has been passed down from generation to generation. The year 1636 is a cruical year in American History. Since 1636, hundreds of thousands of farms have been developed in America. Since American Harvester is built around the farmers, we felt it was only right to credit the history of farms in America. Therefore, the first American Harvester was formed in 1636.
